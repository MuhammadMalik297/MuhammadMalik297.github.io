Metro BI & Dashboards

Spatial analytics and business intelligence dashboards

Problem

Metro systems generate massive amounts of operational data, but they often lack consolidated insights for planning and decision making. The objective of this capstone project was to create a data warehouse for public transit data and design interactive dashboards for stakeholders.

Approach

Using PostgreSQL and PostGIS, we designed a star schema to store ridership, station and fare data. ETL scripts ingested CSV and JSON feeds into the warehouse. Tableau and PowerÂ BI were used to build dashboards showing passenger flows, peak load times and revenue by line. The visualisations allow drilling down by station and time period.

Results

The dashboard suite enables transit authorities to identify bottlenecks, schedule maintenance effectively and optimise fare structures. Decision makers can explore trends interactively and export reports for monthly reviews.
